WhisperQuiet

Hold right Option, talk, and text appears in any app. No account, no subscription, no audio leaves your Mac.

Download for Mac

Apple Silicon / macOS 13.5+ / about 140 MB / unsigned friends preview

A small menu-bar dictation app for the long thoughts you would rather say than type.

What to expect

Works where you type

Hold the hotkey, speak, release. WhisperQuiet types into the app you already have focused.

Private after setup

The speech model downloads once on first launch. After that, dictation runs on-device.

Early, not polished

This is for a few friends to try in real work and tell me where it breaks.

Opening it the first time

The preview is ad-hoc signed, not notarized. macOS will make the first launch look scarier than it is.

  1. Unzip the download and drag WhisperQuiet into Applications.
  2. Right-click the app, choose Open, then choose Open again.
  3. Allow Microphone, Accessibility, and Input Monitoring. Quit and reopen once after granting permissions.
Made for friends. No tracking. If it breaks, that is useful feedback.
